Cabbage Category

KK Cross

KK Cross F1 is a hybrid cabbage compact medium size headed,
pale green color,with globose flattened form,
up to 30 cm in diameter with an average weight of 1.6 kg.
the maturity of the KK cross F1 cabbage is very early,
about 58 days after transplantation.

Watermalon Category

Sugar Baby

This early standar icebox Watermalon, sugar Baby,
is popular troughout the world. this heirloom open pollinated Watermalon
produces round fruit that is 8" in accross and weighing 8 pounds.
The dark green rinds are tough, and the red flesh is great tasting with small black seeds.
the vines ar determinate, and the melons are rip in just 75 days. sugar baby, a 1959 heirloom,
packs a lot of flavor in a small melon, this vairty of watermalon seeds
for planting grows very well in the north, since it is one of the earliest melons available.
